Game Instructions

Objective: Manage an IPL team and bid for players to create the best squad within a set budget.

Squad Requirements

  • Minimum squad size: 18 players
  • Maximum squad size: 24 players
  • Maximum Foreign size: 8 players
  • Roles needed per team:
    • Batsmen (BAT): At least 7
    • Bowlers (BOWL): At least 7
    • Wicketkeepers (WK): At least 3
    • All-rounders (AR): At least 5

Bidding Rules

  • Each player has a base price, which is the starting bid.
  • The auction lasts for 30 seconds per player. If no bids are made within this time, the player remains unsold.
  • Click the "Bid" button to place a bid on a player. Each bid increases the price by â‚¹1 million.
  • Teams compete automatically against your bids. If another team bids higher, the player goes to that team.
  • If your team has the highest bid after 30 seconds, the player is sold to your team and the amount is deducted from your budget.
  • Unsold players will be marked in red, and sold players in green.

How to Play

  1. Select your team and start the auction by clicking "Next Player".
  2. Click "Bid" to place bids. Make sure you have enough budget for each bid.
  3. Try to build a balanced squad that meets the squad requirements.
  4. Once all players are auctioned, the game ends, and you can view your final team lineup.
